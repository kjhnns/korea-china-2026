🐼 Chengdu

Oct 8 to 12 · 4 nights 🏨 Mercure Chengdu Huaxi
Stay
🏨
Mercure Chengdu Huaxi
Our pick · about CHF 50 per night · brand new (opened Aug 2025)
Walk to Jinli Street and Wuhou Temple. Clean and modern, saves roughly CHF 320 to 380 a room over the stay. Splurge alternative: Grand Hyatt Chengdu on Taikoo Li.
Our pick
Activities
🐼
Giant Panda Baby Zone
Chengdu Research Base
Meet the world's most adorable cubs up close in their bamboo paradise. Go at opening time when the pandas are most active.
Activity
Food
🌶️
Authentic Sichuan Hotpot
Chengdu's soul food
A bubbling cauldron of numbing spices, fresh meats and vegetables, best shared with everyone.
Food
🍲
Mapo Tofu at Chen's
The original recipe since 1862
Silken tofu in scorching mala sauce, eaten at the restaurant that invented it.
Food
🥩
Wagyu Hotpot in Wuhou
Michelin selected
Australian M9+ wagyu shoulder cooked tableside in beef-tallow Sichuan broth, 5 minutes from the Wuhou Temple.
Food

🏯 Beijing

Oct 12 to 17 · 5 nights 🏨 Grand Hyatt Beijing
Stay
🏨
Grand Hyatt Beijing
Our pick · about CHF 115 to 120 per night · Wangfujing
Walk to the Forbidden City, matches Grand Hyatt Chengdu for points, same price as the Sofitel. Alternatives: Sofitel Beijing Central, or the Four Seasons kids suite if money is fake.
Our pick
Activities
🏯
The Forbidden City
World's largest palace complex
Walk through 600 years of imperial history across 180 acres of golden-roofed halls.
Activity
🧱
The Great Wall
Mutianyu section, less crowded
Stand atop one of humanity's greatest achievements, ancient stone snaking over wild mountain ridges.
Activity
🛕
Temple of Heaven
Blue-roofed masterpiece
Where emperors prayed for harvests, and where locals do tai chi at dawn every morning.
Activity
⛩️
Summer Palace
290 hectares of imperial lakes
Willow-lined corridors and marble bridges, Beijing's most romantic escape, missed by most tourists.
Activity
🛺
Hutong Neighbourhood Tour
Ancient alleyways
Rickshaws, jianbing street crepes, courtyard homes and rooftop views of the Drum Tower.
Activity
Food
🦆
Peking Duck at Da Dong
The dish Beijing invented
Crackling lacquered skin, tableside carving, gossamer pancakes. The only Peking Duck that matters.
Food
🏮
Sijimingfu, Imperial Duck
Qing dynasty courtyard by the Forbidden City
Four generations of family recipe, hickory smoke ovens, a 108-slice carving ceremony.
Food
🍲
Manhengji, Instant Boiled Mutton
Legendary copper hot pot
Paper-thin Inner Mongolian lamb, legendary sesame dipping sauce, old-school Beijing hospitality.
Food
🍢
Mutton Skewers
Hulunbuir grassland lamb
Beijing's most obsessive lamb skewer chain on a rotating smokeless grill. The Haidilao of BBQ.

Oct 17 to 22 · 5 nights 🏨 Pullman Jing An
Getting there
🚄
Bullet Train, Business Class
Beijing to Shanghai in 4.5h at 350 km/h
Lie-flat seats, meal service, panoramic views. Better than flying in every way.
Transport
Stay
🏨
Pullman Jing An
Our pick · about CHF 90 per night · central Jing'an
Top metro connection, best value in Shanghai, about half the cost of the Hyatt on the Bund. Splurge alternative: Hyatt on the Bund for the river and skyline view.
Our pick
Activities
🌆
The Bund Waterfront
Shanghai's most iconic panorama
A mile-long promenade of colonial grandeur facing the futuristic Pudong skyline.
Activity
🏞️
Yu Garden and Old City
16th-century classical garden
Moon gates, koi ponds and dragon walls, plus the original soup dumpling teahouse on the lotus pond.
Activity
🌃
Shanghai Tower, Floor 118
World's 2nd tallest building, 632m up
Above the clouds, looking down on the whole city and out to the East China Sea.
Activity
🌳
The French Concession
Tree-lined Art Deco streets
Independent cafes, Xintiandi and Tianzifang. The neighbourhood that makes you want to move to Shanghai.
Activity
🛳️
Huangpu River Night Cruise
Both skylines from the water at night
The Bund and Pudong glowing, reflections on the river. The best single view in Shanghai.
Activity
🚣
Zhujiajiao Water Town
Ming dynasty canal town 30 km out
Gondolas, 36 stone bridges and zero skyscrapers.
Activity
🏰
Shanghai Disneyland
Newest, most spectacular Disney park on earth
TRON coaster, the world's largest castle, a fireworks show over the moat. One for the kids.
Activity
🧱
Shanghai LEGOLAND Resort
World's largest LEGOLAND, opened July 2025
Eight themed lands, MINILAND China in millions of bricks, rides and a themed hotel. A full day for the kids.
Activity
Food
🥟
Nanxiang Xiaolongbao
The original 1900 soup dumpling house in Yu Garden
18 hand-pleated folds, scalding broth inside. The benchmark every other dumpling is judged against.
Food
🥢
